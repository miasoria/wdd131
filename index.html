Santiago del Estero, Argentina

Did you know that Argentina is home to the widest avenue in the world? Avenida 9 de Julio, located in the heart of Buenos Aires, has up to 16 lanes of traffic and stretches over 140 meters wide! Named after Argentina’s Independence Day, this iconic avenue features landmarks like the Obelisk and is a symbol of the country’s vibrant culture and love for grand expressions. It’s a place where history, architecture, and the fast pace of city life all collide—just like tango on asphalt.
